Poem - Amazing Messi

A short poem on Lionel Messi

He really does rare magic. many wonders, you may all have seen, but since his evolution! every gloves panic. For he is a stunning little bee, who possesses myriads of tricks. He's no one but the amazing Messi, one who the Catalonians call the ‘Messiah’ and who to me, shows no iota of mercy while on the rich green pitch. Messi's not just an ordinary star, he's a super being and an iconic figure. I call him a modern day art, gracing each and every soccer fixtures.

Ronny had prophesized in Nou Camp about someone, a little man who will lead Catalonians home. this, many had dubbed a mere fiction but here now we live in the revelation. Messi’s kick takes control of every souls, even his enemies celebrate his goals. His sublime kicks stun many from the bench, even King Pele can't help but nod his head. dedicated to the greatest footballer of all time, Messi. he is a blessing to the soccer world. I am glad he came in my time.
